Graphics Design.

6 Weeks Onsite/Virtual Training

The Graphics design course was designed to guide you from the basic to the professional level of graphics designing and corporate branding. With a combination of the trainer led sessions and self study, practical tasks and projects, learning will not only be talkshops, but workshops, which will provide you with the opportunity of demonstrating your expertise and building your portfolio, even while learning. Upon completion of the learning program, you will be certified and job-ready as a professional graphics and branding expert.

Eligibility

  • You must be ready to dedicate 6 weeks (weekdays or weekend), whether onsite or online, to active learning and practice. Perhaps, you are a busy person, contact us directly to design a custom learning timetable for you.
