Why work for us?



At Kwara Tech Academy, we strongly believe in the ability of technology to enhance organisation and their processes, and the individual lives.


We develop programmes that help individual build quality and high paying tech careers, and at the same time, help organisations build teams of diverse of diverse tech talent.


We are solution and result driven, our vision is to suffieciently empower 30,000 young tech innovators and trainers, making a definite shift in tech sector, with the teams we build. With our partnerships and sponsorship, we are doggedly working towards crushing every barriers to our next generation tech talent, supporting them through to becoming the next generation tech giants and world problem solvers


We do not believe in ineuality of any form. This alone has driven us to organise work shops and empowerment programmes for kids and women in tech, helping us to build inclusive tech teams of diverse background, perspective and expertise for better and more innovative work.


We will stop at nothing in our pursuit to closing the digital gap and empowering passionate individuals in tech.
